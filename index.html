About Me

I graduated from an art school and am a trained media designer for digital and print.
I have been at home in the world of UI/UX and Graphic design since 2019. Alongside my full-time job, I completed my Bachelor of Science degree in "Digital Media".
‍
My creative spectrum is diverse:
UI/UX design, app and web design, graphic design, print design and illustration as well as merchandise design are my strengths.
‍
In my spare time, I can be found at concerts and love to travel. My love of languages drives me to deepen my knowledge of Spanish, Italian and Portuguese.

Want to know more? Send me an email and let's meet up for a coffee - wether online or in person.
Let's create something extraordinary together!
